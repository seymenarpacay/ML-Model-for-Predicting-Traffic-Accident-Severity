# ML-Model-for-Predicting-Traffic-Accident-Severity
Main Objective: To develop a model that predicts the "Most Severe Injury" in an accident using past accident records. Problem Type: A Classification problem within the scope of Supervised Learning..

Please enter the entries in English and in capital letters as shown.

==================== USER INPUT INFORMATION ====================

1) Traffic Control (traffic_control_device)
- NO CONTROLS (Most Common)
- TRAFFIC SIGNAL
- STOP SIGN/FLASHER
- YIELD
- LANE USE MARKING
- RAILROAD CROSSING GATE
- SCHOOL ZONE
- POLICE/FLAGMAN
- OTHER
- UNKNOWN

2) Crash Type (first_crash_type)
- REAR END
- TURNING Collision)
- PARKED MOTOR VEHICLE
- FIXED OBJECT
- PEDESTRIAN
- SIDESWIPE SAME DIRECTION
- ANGLE
- HEAD ON
- ANIMAL
- PEDALCYCLIST

3) Weather Conditions
- CLEAR
- RAIN
- SNOW
- CLOUDY/OVERCAST
- FOG/SMOKE/HAZE
- SLEET/HAIL
- FREEZING RAIN/DRIZZLE
- BLOWING SNOW (Type)

4) Lighting (lighting_condition)
- DAYLIGHT
- DARKNESS (Darkness - No Light)
- DARKNESS, LIGHTED ROAD (Darkness - Street Lamped)
- DUSK (Darkness)
- DAWN (Dawn)

5) Primary Contributory Cause (prim_contributory_cause)
- UNABLE TO DETERMINE
- FAILING TO YIELD RIGHT-OF-WAY
- FOLLOWING TOO CLOSELY
- IMPROPER OVERTAKING/PASSING
- FAILING TO REDUCE SPEED
- IMPROPER TURNING/NO SIGNAL
- DRIVING SKILLS/KNOWLEDGE/EXPERIENCE (Driving Inexperience)
- WEATHER
- DISREGARDING TRAFFIC SIGNALS
- DISREGARDING STOP SIGN
- OPERATING VEHICLE IN ERRATIC MANNER
- PHYSICAL CONDITION OF DRIVER
- DISTRACTION FROM INSIDE VEHICLE
- EQUIPMENT - VEHICLE CONDITION

6) Road Type (trafficway_type)
- NOT DIVIDED
- DIVIDED - W/MEDIAN
- ONE-WAY
- FOUR-WAY
- INTERSECTION (T-Intersection)
- PARKING LOT
- ROUNDABOUT
- RAMP

7) Road Surface (roadway_surface_cond)
- DRY
- WET
- SNOW OR SLUSH
- ICE
- SAND, MUD, DIRT

8) Alignment
- STRAIGHT AND LEVEL
- CURVE, LEVEL
- STRAIGHT ON GRADE
- CURVE ON GRADE

9) Number of Vehicles (num_units)
- Enter an integer between 1 and 10. Example: 1, 2, 5, 10

10) Day of the Week
- Enter an integer between 1 and 7
1 = Monday
2 = Tuesday
3 = Wednesday
4 = Thursday
5 = Friday
6 = Saturday
7 = Sunday

11) Crash Hour
- Enter an integer between 1 and 24

12) Intersection Related?
Enter 1 or 0.
